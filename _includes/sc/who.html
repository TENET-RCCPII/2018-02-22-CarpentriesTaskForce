Who: The meeting is organised for volunteers who stepped forward to join an African Carpentry Task Force in 2018. Calls for participation went out to all African Carpentry instructors and trainees and was also discussed in the African Carpentry Instructor Meetup in 2017. The task force currently consist of African-based instructors who have indicated an interest to support Carpentry workshops in South Africa and the rest of Africa. The task force will be run as a pilot project for 2018 and feedback will be provided to the community about the lessons learned. All task force members are qualified, active Carpentry instructors.
